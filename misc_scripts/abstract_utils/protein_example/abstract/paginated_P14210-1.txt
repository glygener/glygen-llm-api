HGF exhibits significant glycosylation activity, notably with four N-linked glycosylation sites, specifically Asn294, Asn402, Asn566, and Asn653. For instance, the single nucleotide variant (SNV) at position 39 is linked to colorectal cancer. Regarding biological processes, top Gene Ontology annotations point to its roles in cell chemotaxis, cell morphogenesis, and epithelial to mesenchymal transition, encapsulating its significant impact in oncogenesis.
